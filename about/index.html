About

August 14, 2024

Hey, I’m Taha, a freshman at Carnegie Mellon University majoring in Computer Science. I have a deep passion for cybersecurity, with a focus on application security, penetration testing, and low-level security like reverse engineering and binary exploitation.

I’ve had the chance to play CTFs with MMM/PPP internationally and NEDİM KURU in Turkey.

A few years back, I responsibly disclosed vulnerabilities to over 30 organizations, including Apple and the U.S. Department of Defense. See a list of these at /acknowledgements.

If you’re into hacking, math or just want to talk shop, feel free to reach out at linkedin, x, or tbiyikli at andrew dot cmu.
